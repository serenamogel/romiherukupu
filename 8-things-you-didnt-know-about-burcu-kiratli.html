8 Things You Didnt Know About Burcu Kiratli

March 2024 · 3 minute read
Burcu Kiratli was born in the year 1989 on 23rd July in Istanbul, Tukey. The most talented actress, Burcu is a Turkish actress and model. This diva is known for her flawless beauty and acting, her journey started in the year 2010. She has won awards for her roles and also has performed in the famous movie which was based on a real story. Burcu went through a roller coaster in her love life yet has a great journey in her film career. Still, there is much more to know about her. Let us look into the unknown facts of this beautiful woman.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Burcu Kiratli

1- Got Massive Appreciation

Burcu Kiratli did not start off her career just like that as a minor in fact while completing her education she took several short movie projects and took part in theatre plays. Burcu Kiratli then made her debut on television in the series Elde Var Hayat, which gained her massive appreciation.

2- Holds a Bachelors Degree

Burcu Kiratli did her primary education at Ata College, then her secondary education from BJK College after completing high school she graduated from the Yeditepe University and holds a bachelors degree.

3- Remarried to Hubby after Divorce

Kiratli was born in Turkish family to Gul Ercetingoz and Tuncay Kiratli. After completing her education she married Sinan Akcil also of Turkish origin. Her husband and she got divorced in 2019 and then remarried her husband in 2021, the separation time was indeed a roller coaster in her life.

4- Steps in the Drama Industry

Burcu Kiratli made her debut on television in the series Elde Var Hayat as Yagmur which earned her the first fan base, this series lasted for 2 seasons, and then her debut in her first film in the year 2013 as Helin film named Su ve Ates.

5- First Success

Burcu played the character of Peri in the film Peri Masali back in 2014. The critical success of this movie and her performance gained her as one of the best in her characters so far that she played as in the movies.

6- Achievements

Burcu Kiratli earned the best actress award for her drama and movies, in 2015 for Peri Masali and then for the same movie in 2018 which was elected as the best movie of the year 2000, also Best actress award for Dirilis Ertugrul, and then the most prestigious Award was given her to by President Erdogan for Best actress of the year.

7- Contribution and Appreciation

Burcu Kiratli no doubt worked hard in her acting career and then after the massive success she garnered an Award for being the Most Stylish Actress of the year 2019-2020 which was given her to by the Istanbul University Fashion Society for her continuous contribution to the Turkish Cinema Industry in 2008.

8- Fitness and Beauty

Actresses are known for their beauty and spot-on acting which comes from continuous self-care and daily workouts to concentrate in the field they are in. Her workout includes swimming, walking her daily balanced diet which can be noted through her social media.
