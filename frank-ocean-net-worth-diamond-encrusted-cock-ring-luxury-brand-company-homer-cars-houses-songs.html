What is Frank Oceans net worth? Rappers brand Homer launches $25K diamond-encrusted gold penis

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Frank Ocean only makes the headlines when he wants to and the rapper has once again managed to raise the eyebrows after his luxury brand company Homer announced a new line of accessories that includes pendants, earrings, a keychain, and a diamond-encrusted penis ring. The newly launched ring has been dubbed “XXXL H-Bone Ring" and is priced at a whopping $25K.
 
The 18-karat gold ring was promoted on the Instagram page of Frank Ocean’s company where a Black man’s torso is visible while wearing the ring with their genitals blurred out. It is unclear if the 34-year-old artist posed for the photo but it was captioned as, “PHOTO: frank “PACO” ocean.” The press release of Ocean’s new luxury product stated that it “represents carving history into stone”, as reported by Complex. The ‘Channel Orange’ hitmaker’s brand was launched in 2021.

What is Frank Ocean’s net worth?

From ghostwriting for top global artists to songwriting and then carving his own path as a singer and rapper, Frank Ocean cemented his position in the industry as one of the most creative minds. The California native might have dropped only four albums since his debut in 2011 but they are all considered as some of the most iconic projects ever released.
 
Frank Ocean, who shot to fame with hits like ‘Ivy’, Pyramids’, ‘Pink+White’, ‘Chanel’, ‘Nights’, and others is known for his compelling lyrics and unorthodox sound. He is a two-time Grammy winner and in 2013 was listed as 100 most influential people in the world by Times Magazine. Speaking of his net worth, Frank Ocean is valued at an impressive $13 million, as reported by Celebrity Net Worth. He takes home fat pay cheques from streaming revenues, album sales, concert tours, his luxury brand company Homer and lucrative brand endorsements.    

Frank Ocean’s stunning Malibu ranch

Frank Ocean, in 2019, secretly bought a ranch in Malibu and shelled out a cool $6.35 million for the same. The property was earlier leased out to former ‘Real Housewives of New Jersey’ cast member Dina Manzo who owned the place for over 50 years.

The stunning ranch sprawled across 1.5 acres was built in 1955. It flaunts a two-car garage, an eat-in kitchen with custom cabinetry, a carpeted dining room, a family room with an oversized fireplace along with five bedrooms, a built-in soaking tub and walk-in closet, and a vaulted ceiling, as published by The Dirt. The house comes with breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and Catalina Island.     

Frank Ocean’s obsession with cars

Apart from his music, Frank Ocean is obsessed with cars. The renowned R&B singer is known for getting customized cars for his albums. His studio projects have even featured four-wheelers on the covers. According to Atelier Eau Rouge, Frank’s very first car from his music money was a BMW 550i. He later invested in a 1991 Civic and a 2002 Nissan Maxima.
 
According to the publication, after the success of his mixtape ‘Nostalgia, Ultra’ and debut studio album ‘Channel Orange’, the award-winning crooner parked a few luxurious BMW rides in his garage like E30 M3, E90 M3 and an F10 M5. E46 and E30s were also later added to his ever-growing collection. A Tesla Model X, McLaren 675LT, and a Porsche GT3 are some other cars Frank Ocean loves to drive.
