Spice Market Buffet at Planet Hollywood: Prices, Hours &amp; Menu In 2022

Spice Market Buffet

Spice Market Buffet: In an apparent reference to its earlier Middle Eastern theme as the Aladdin Hotel & Casino, the Planet Hollywood Hotel & Casino located in Las Vegas has a Middle-eastern themed buffet known as”Spice Market. “Spice Market Buffet.”

In past years the buffet was regarded as one of the top buffets as per an annual “Best of Las Vegas” poll conducted by The Las Vegas Review-Journal.

This isn’t this due to several brand new and upgraded buffets at casinos along the Strip and a general decrease in the offerings on The Spice Market.

The Spice Market is primarily known for its Middle Eastern/Mediterranean section. It is among the buffets that are included in Caesars Entertainment’s Buffet of Buffets Pass which allows you to pay one price to gain admission to several buffets during a 24-hour time period.

It’s among the stations available there, as well as the American Station, Asian, Soup, Seafood, Mexican, Salad bar, pastry shop, and, obviously, dessert.

It is located on the lower level on the lower level PH located on the lower level at PH, the Spice Market is pretty affordable in comparison to its better buffets located across the Strip at the Bellagio and Caesars Palace.

As of the month of February 2020 in 2020, the Spice Market began offering no breakfast buffet. There’s no longer an option for a lunch or breakfast buffet only a brunch on weekends.

Menu/Food

There are fewer food outlets than modern buffets, however, they do include Mexican, Middle Eastern, and American stations. There are also salads, soups, and sweets.

The top items in Spice Market are the carving stations, sugar-free cakes as well as cotton candy that is hard to get at all buffets.

Take a look at their Tri-Tip and Tandoori Skewers. Another option is Their Middle Eastern and Mediterranean dishes provide vegan options.

Dinner

Dinner menus will feature the majority of the lunch options as well as these meals that aren’t served during brunch such as shrimp scampi pasta Crab-stuffed soles, seafood cioppino as well as barbecue pork ribs.

The buffet dinner has been changed to “Prime Rib Dinner” and features (as you’d guess) prime rib is served every evening. There’s also a selection of tri-tips, turkey roasted, and barbecue chicken.

The dessert station doesn’t make a splash, since it is stocked with the typical selection of cookies, sweets cupcakes, cakes, etc.

Drinks

FAQs

Are there Vegan options at Spice Market?

Yes, they do. Mediterranean menu along with their Middle Eastern station has several vegan alternatives. Ask the server at the buffet at every station.

Do they take reservations?

Not unless you’re hosting an extremely large gathering. For parties with 12 or more, please call (866) 733-5827, or (702) 731-7778.

Can I buy the Buffet of Buffets pass at Spice Market?

Yes, you can purchase this Buffet of Buffets pass at any of the participating buffets.
