List of Pokmon Adventures XY volumes

February 2024 · 2 minute read

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

If you were looking for the volumes published in English by VIZ Media, see List of Pokémon XY volumes.

Due to chapters taking at least two years before being collected into a volume, the X & Y arc of Pokémon Adventures was separately collected into mini-volumes by Shogakukan in Japan, which began in April 2014 and ended in December 2016. The chapters included in these mini-volumes were only ones that were published in the magazine CoroCoro Ichiban. Once the Black 2 & White 2 arc ended in the regular volumes, the X & Y arc started to be properly collected in that format as well.

These volumes have also been localized in English by Shogakukan Asia, in European French by Kurokawa, in German by Panini Comics and in European Spanish by Norma Editorial.
