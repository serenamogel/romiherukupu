Sluice Gate Vault in BG3

The Sluice Gate serves as a substantial entrance into a sewage conduit, accessible through a unique mechanism. To unlock this gate, one must engage with various valves positioned on a platform situated above ground level. This interactive process entails manipulating the valves to regulate water and temperature levels.

Successfully coordinating these valves prompts the gate to open, granting access to the sewage pipe beyond. The valves' careful manipulation is necessary to avoid the release of harmful elements from the gauges. Ultimately, the task demands precision in synchronizing the valves' actions to achieve the desired effect. This dynamic interaction introduces an intriguing challenge in gaining entry through the Sluice Gate and venturing further into the sewage pipe.

How to Open the Sluice Gate vault in BG3?

To open the Sluice Gate vault in Baldur's Gate 3, follow these detailed steps:

Locate the Note: First, make your way to the platform above the Sluice Gate. Here, you'll find a note attached to one of the pipes. This note provides crucial information on how to open the vault.

Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

Understand the Mechanism: The note explains that the water and temperature valves need to be adjusted simultaneously to their optimal levels for the Sluice Gate to open. There are two valves on the platform: the water valve on the left and the temperature valve on the right.

Observe the Gauges: Next to each valve, there's a gauge displaying two colors, blue/red and green. Your objective is to align the pointers of both gauges within the middle of the green section.

Execute the Process:

a. Water Valve: Begin with a single character. Interact with the water valve by clicking it once. This action starts the water gauge's ascent, and a timer is displayed on the valve.

b. Timing: Wait for a few seconds to let the water gauge rise gradually. Take note of the speed at which the gauge progresses.

c. Temperature Valve: Move your character to the temperature valve after the water gauge has been activated. Click the temperature valve once to initiate the temperature gauge's rise.

d. Synchronization: Because the temperature gauge increases faster than the water gauge, the goal is to time your actions in such a way that both gauges reach their optimal levels (the middle of the green space) at the same time.

Be Cautious: During this process, be mindful of the gauges' progression. Prevent them from reaching the extreme ends of their scales. If they do, they'll release water or fire that can damage your characters.

Reset Valve: Should you need to start over, you can use the reset valve situated on the far right of the platform to reset the gauges.

Persistence and Success: It might take several attempts to get the timing right. Keep experimenting with the sequence and timing of valve activation.

Vault Opens: Once you successfully synchronize both the water and temperature gauges to the green level simultaneously, the Sluice Gate vault will open. Proceed through the gate, but be prepared to face enemies on the other side.

Baldur's Gate 3

Baldur's Gate III is a role-playing video game that was developed and published by Larian Studios. As the third main installment in the Baldur's Gate series, it continues the legacy of this iconic franchise, which is based on the popular Dungeons & Dragons tabletop role-playing system.

The game was initially released in a partial version as an early access format for macOS, Windows, and the Stadia streaming service on October 6, 2020. This early access allowed players to experience and test the game while it was still in development, providing feedback to the developers to improve and refine the gameplay.

The early access period continued for nearly three years, during which Larian Studios actively worked on enhancing the game's features, fixing bugs, and implementing player suggestions. The full release of Baldur's Gate III for PC was finally launched on August 3, 2023, marking the game's official completion and readiness for a broader audience.

Following the PC release, plans were made for a subsequent release on other platforms. The PlayStation 5 version was scheduled to be released on September 6, 2023, allowing console players to also experience the epic journey of Baldur's Gate III. Additionally, an Xbox Series X/S port was also under development, indicating the game's commitment to reach a wider console audience. However, specific details about the Xbox release date were not mentioned in the provided information.

Unfortunately, the Stadia version of the game faced a setback, as it was cancelled due to the closure of the Stadia streaming service. This decision might have disappointed Stadia users who were looking forward to playing the game on this platform.

Baldur's Gate 3 Gameplay

Baldur's Gate III is a role-playing video game that offers a captivating gameplay experience with both single-player and cooperative multiplayer elements. Players have the freedom to create one or more characters and assemble a party, which can include computer-generated characters, to embark on an epic adventure within the game's rich and immersive story.

In the single-player mode, players take control of their created character or characters and explore the vast world of Baldur's Gate III on their own. They will encounter a wide array of challenges, make critical decisions, and interact with various non-player characters (NPCs) that shape the course of their journey. The single-player mode allows players to fully immerse themselves in the game's narrative and experience the unfolding story at their own pace.

Additionally, the game offers a cooperative multiplayer mode that enhances the social aspect of the gameplay. In this mode, players have the option to team up with friends or other players online, bringing their individual characters into a shared party. By combining forces, they can tackle challenges, quests, and battles together, fostering a sense of camaraderie and cooperation throughout their adventure.

The combat system in Baldur's Gate III has undergone significant changes compared to its predecessors. Instead of the real-time combat found in earlier games in the series, Baldur's Gate III adopts a turn-based combat system. This shift was inspired by Larian's previous successful titles, Divinity: Original Sin and Divinity: Original Sin II. Players now engage in strategic, tactical battles, where they can plan their moves carefully and execute actions based on the D&D 5th Edition rules.

The implementation of the D&D 5th Edition ruleset ensures that the game adheres closely to the beloved tabletop role-playing system. This enables players to use familiar mechanics, spells, abilities, and character stats, making the transition from the tabletop to the digital realm seamless for fans of the Dungeons & Dragons franchise.
