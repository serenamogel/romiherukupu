Meri Brown Biography

Meri Brown is an American Reality Tv Personality, Author, and Businesswoman who is well-known as Kody Brown’s Wife. She is also known for appearing in TLC’s “Sister Wives” reality series. The show’s first season came out in 2010 and by 2018 the show had hit 13 seasons. To further explain, the show is based on her husband Kody Brown who has four wives and shares 18 kids with his companions.

Meri Brown Age

How old is Meri? Meri is 51 years old as of 2022. She was born on January 16, 1971, in Alameda, California, the United States of America. In addition, she celebrates her birthday on January 16 every year and her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Meri Brown Height

Meri stands at an average height of 5 feet 7 inches and is of moderate weight weighing 70 kg equivalent to 154 lbs. She has blue eyes and blonde hair. In addition, her other body measurements are 38-32-36 inches.

Meri Brown Family

Meri is the proud daughter of William James Barber (father) and Bonnie Ahlstrom (mother). Her father died on September 5, 2007. She has six siblings, four sisters Teresa Barber Kunz, Rebekah Barber Dilts, Deborah Barber Bronson, and Elaine Barber Jenson, and two brothers, Adam Barber and Marc William Barber.

Meri Brown Husband | Kody Brown

Meri married Kody Brown on April 21, 1990. Kody has four wives and shares 18 children with them. This huge family was off the spotlight before their lives became a reality program. On the other hand, Kody and Janelle were blessed with 8 children, Michelle, Logan, Maddie, Caleb, Hunter, Garrison, Gabriel, and Savanah.
