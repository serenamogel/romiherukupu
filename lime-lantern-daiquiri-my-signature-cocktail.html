Lime Lantern Daiquiri

Lime Lantern Daiquiri – A tantalizing daiquiri recipe made with fresh watermelon, lime juice and BACARDI® Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix.

Course Beverages Cuisine American Keyword lime lantern daiquiri Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 6 Calories 148kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 10 oz Frozen BACARDI® Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri Mix 1 can
  • 1 cup BACARDI® Gold
  • 1 1/2 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 4 cups ice
  • 3 limes for garnishes
  • 6 tsp BACARDI® 151

Instructions

  • Cut 3 limes in half. Use a citrus press to juice the limes. Reserve the lime shells and juice.

  • Add all ingredients to a blender EXCEPT for the garnishes and blend on high for approximately 20 seconds or until smooth.

  • Pour into six cocktail or martini glasses.

  • Take the lime shells and push them inside out to create a "lime bowl".

  • Float each shell in the center of the glass being careful to keep the lime above the cocktail.

  • Add 1 tsp of BACARDI® 151 to the "lime bowl" and use a long-stemmed lighter to ignite the rum.

  • Dimming the lights will help with the effect.

  • Make sure flames have been extinguished before drinking.

  • Enjoy!
