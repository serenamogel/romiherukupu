Jamie Campbell Bower Movies, Age, Height, Band, Songs, Family, Wiki

James Metcalfe Campbell Bower is a singer and actor from England. Bower received critical acclaim in 2022 for his performance as Henry Creel in the fourth season of Stranger Things.

Jamie Campbell Bower Movies

Jamie Campbell Bower’s movie credits include The Twilight Saga, The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 and Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald.

Jamie Campbell Bower Age

How old is Jamie Campbell Bower? He was born on 22 November 1988 in London, England. He is 33 years old.

Jamie Campbell Bower Height

How tall is Jamie Campbell Bower? He is 1.83 m tall

Jamie Campbell Bower Band

Is Jamie Campbell Bower in a band? He was the frontman of the London-based band Counterfeit from 2015 to 2020.

Jamie Campbell Bower Songs

Does Jamie Campbell Bower have any songs? He has released 5 singles since leaving his band. Paralysed, Start the Fire, Crow, May Run On (feat. King Sugar) and Devil In Me

Jamie Campbell Bower Family

Bower’s mother works as a music manager, and his father works at the Gibson Guitar Corporation. Sir John Campbell of Airds and Lieutenant Governor of Saint Vincent was his maternal great-great-great-great-grandfather.
